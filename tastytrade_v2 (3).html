2 Churn Prediction

Traders who lose money
go quiet. Stop finding out too late.

The problem right now
A new trader funds, places a few trades, takes losses on a bad week — and stops logging in. By the time you know they're gone, they've already defunded. The window to bring them back has closed.

Amplitude identifies at-risk traders days before they defund — based on behavioral signals, not gut feel. Funded, traded, negative P&L, login frequency dropping. That cohort is recoverable with the right message at the right moment.

  • Identify at-risk traders based on behavior — not calendar-based email cadences
  • See the exact session where engagement dropped and what preceded it
  • Trigger re-engagement based on P&L, login frequency, and feature usage signals
  • Measure whether interventions actually change downstream trading behavior
